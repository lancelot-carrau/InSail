Our Mission

At InSail, we believe maritime mobility can become a vector for ocean regeneration. Our mission is to transform an industry that is often noisy, polluting, and disconnected from the environment into a system that respects and preserves marine biodiversity.

We develop hybrid maritime transport solutions that combine the power of wind with clean technologies, drastically reducing environmental impact while maintaining performance and reliability.

Innovation & Regeneration

Our approach is based on three fundamental pillars:

  • • Circular Economy - Recycled and recyclable materials
  • • Biomimicry - Learning from nature to innovate
  • • Positive Impact - Regenerating marine ecosystems
